Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us and getting support

What are your support hours?

Our support team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Indian Standard Time (IST). For urgent matters outside these hours, you can email us and we'll respond as soon as possible.

How quickly will I get a response to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For technical support issues, our team typically responds within 4-8 hours, depending on the complexity of the issue.

Do you offer on-site training or implementation support?

Yes, we offer on-site training and implementation support for enterprise customers. This service is available for educational institutions throughout India. Please contact us directly to discuss your specific requirements and to get a customized quote.

How can I report a technical issue or bug?

To report a technical issue or bug, please use the contact form on this page and select "Technical Support" from the subject dropdown. Provide as much detail as possible, including your browser version, device, and steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots or screen recordings are also very helpful.

Can I schedule a demo of the platform?

Absolutely! We'd be happy to provide a personalized demo of MKCE EXAM EXAM PORTAL for your organization. Please use the contact form and select "General Inquiry" from the subject dropdown, mentioning that you're interested in a demo. Alternatively, you can call us directly to schedule a convenient time.
