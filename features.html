Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is the platform for high-stakes exams?

MK EXAM PORTAL employs multiple layers of security, including AI-powered proctoring with facial recognition, browser lockdown technology that prevents access to unauthorized resources, IP monitoring to detect suspicious location changes, and randomized question banks to prevent cheating. Our system is designed to meet the security requirements for high-stakes examinations and certification tests.

Can I import questions from other platforms?

Yes, MK EXAM PORTAL supports importing questions from various formats including QTI, Blackboard, Canvas, Moodle, and Excel spreadsheets. Our import wizard guides you through the process of mapping your existing questions to our platform's format, allowing you to migrate your content without starting from scratch.

How does the analytics system help improve teaching?

Our analytics dashboard provides detailed insights into student performance at individual, class, and institutional levels. You can identify common misconceptions through item analysis, track progress over time, detect knowledge gaps, and compare performance across different cohorts. These insights help you refine your teaching strategies, focus on challenging areas, and provide targeted interventions for struggling students.

Is the platform accessible for students with disabilities?

Yes, MK EXAM PORTAL is designed with accessibility in mind and complies with WCAG 2.1 guidelines. We support screen readers, keyboard navigation, high-contrast mode, and text resizing. The platform also allows for accommodations such as extended time, breaks during exams, and alternative formats for content presentation to ensure all students have equal access to assessment opportunities.

How can I integrate MK EXAM PORTAL with my existing LMS?

We offer seamless integration with popular Learning Management Systems including Canvas, Blackboard, Moodle, and Brightspace. The integration allows for single sign-on, automatic roster syncing, and grade passback. For Enterprise customers, we also offer API access for custom integrations with proprietary systems.
