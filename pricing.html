Frequently Asked Questions

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan at any time?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time, and the changes will take effect immediately. If you need to downgrade, the change will apply at the end of your current billing cycle. There are no penalties for changing plans.

How does billing work for student accounts?

Our pricing is based on the number of admin accounts (teachers, instructors, or test administrators) rather than students. Each plan includes a specific number of student accounts that can be assigned to your exams. If you need additional student capacity, you can add more at a nominal cost per additional 100 students.

Do you offer discounts for educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for schools, colleges, and universities. We provide a 25% discount for verified educational institutions. Please contact our sales team with your official institutional email to verify eligibility.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and net banking options. For Enterprise plans, we also support purchase orders and direct bank transfers. For educational institutions in India, we also accept UPI payments and can provide GST invoices for your records.

Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all plans with full access to features. No credit card is required to start your trial. At the end of your trial period, you can select the plan that best fits your needs or reach out to our sales team for a customized solution.
